Secure Your Crypto Connections

Trézor Bridge®™ is the key to connecting your Trézor® hardware wallet seamlessly to your computer. Acting as a secure communication layer, Trézor Bridge® enables your desktop applications and web browsers to interact safely with your wallet, keeping your crypto transactions secure at every step.

Without Trézor Bridge®, your wallet may not be recognized properly by your computer, limiting your ability to send or receive digital assets. Installing Trézor Bridge® takes just minutes, offering a smooth and simple setup process. Once installed, it runs quietly in the background, ensuring reliable and encrypted communication between your device and trusted applications.

Trézor Bridge®™ is supported on Windows, macOS, and Linux, making it accessible to crypto enthusiasts across the globe. Its open-source nature ensures transparency, giving you confidence that your data and private keys always remain in your hands.

Experience the ease of managing your crypto securely with Trézor Bridge®. Download it today and make your Trézor® wallet connection smoother and safer than ever before.
